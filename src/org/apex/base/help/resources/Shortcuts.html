Full or partial withdrawal of your Term Deposit is at the discretion of ANZ.

Where ANZ agrees to an early withdrawal, the money you withdraw early will earn 50% of the interest rate that was originally agreed, and the money you leave on your term deposit will continue to earn the interest rate originally agreed.

A partial withdrawal is only possible if the amount remaining in the Term Deposit will not be less than the minimum balance allowed; otherwise the entire balance must be withdrawn.
