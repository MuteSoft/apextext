Important Information

When you request a future-dated payment/transfer, please remember that cleared funds must be available in your account at 12.00am (midnight AEST) on the day before the scheduled payment/transfer date or your payment will be unsuccessful.

You must ensure that the EFT Code is correct or your payment may be unsuccessful or may be paid to an unintended account.

Tax payments requested before 6.00pm (AEST) on a Banking Business Day will be processed to your account on the same day. Transfers requested after this time may be processed on the next Banking Business Day. When the Australian Taxation Office's account will be credited, will depend on the policy and systems of the Reserve Bank of Australia.

You cannot cancel or delete immediate payments or a payment to be processed today. To delete a future-dated bill payment, go to Future Payments/Transfers. Bill Payments must be deleted prior to 12.00am (midnight AEST) on the day before the scheduled payment date.
